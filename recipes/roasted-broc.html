Broccoli Recipe

Description

Broccoli is great, it's a veggie with many nutrients. When it's roasted it smacks so hard.

Ingredients

  • Head of broccoli
  • Olive oil
  • dash of salt

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 425
  2. Toss broccoli in oil and salt
  3. Cook in oven for about 20 minutes
  4. serve it up boss
