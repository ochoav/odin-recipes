Rice Recipe

Description

Rice always smack, its traditional side dish in many countries. I love to eat it with some salmon or chicken or anything really.

Today we will make classic white rice, for that meal prep baby. It's like 300 cals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1.25 cups of water
  • dash of salt

Steps

  1. Rinse rice
  2. Mix with cup of water
  3. Bring to boil
  4. Lower to simmer and cover
  5. Cook for 15 minutes dO NO DISTURB
  6. Uncover, fluff, and serve
